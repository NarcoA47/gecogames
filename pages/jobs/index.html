Application Process

1. Submit Your Application

Send us your resume and a cover letter. Tell us why you're passionate about gaming and why you'd be a great fit.

2. Interview with Our Team

We’ll set up interviews to get to know you better and see how your skills align with our needs.

3. Final Interview & Offer

You'll have a final interview with senior management, and if all goes well, we’ll extend an offer.

4. Join GecoGames!

Welcome aboard! You'll start with an orientation and meet your new team. Let the adventure begin!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application process like?

The application process typically involves submitting your resume, a series of interviews, and a final interview with senior management.

What benefits do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive benefits package, including health insurance, retirement plans, wellness programs, and more.

Can I apply for more than one position?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions. Make sure to tailor your resume and cover letter for each role.

How can I prepare for an interview?

Research GecoGames, review the job description, and be ready to discuss how your skills and experience align with the role.
