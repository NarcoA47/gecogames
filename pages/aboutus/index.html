Our Mission

To revolutionize the Zambian game development industry by creating culturally enriched video games that educate, inspire, and entertain. We strive to promote and preserve the diverse cultures and languages of Zambia, while empowering the younger generation to understand and build upon the cultural legacy passed down through generations.

Our Values

Cultural Preservation and Innovation: Geco Games Studios values the preservation and celebration of Zambia's rich cultural heritage. We believe in fostering diversity by incorporating various cultures and languages into our video games. Through our work, we aim to innovate and create meaningful gaming experiences that resonate with players while honoring the traditions and values of our communities.

Our Objectives

  • Promote Cultural Awareness: Develop video games that integrate and celebrate Zambia’s diverse cultures and languages, ensuring that each game is a platform for cultural education and appreciation.
  • Innovate in Game Development: Lead the Zambian game development industry by pioneering new methods, technologies, and creative approaches that set a standard for quality and innovation in the region.
  • Empower the Next Generation: Educate and mentor young Zambians, providing them with the knowledge and skills needed to understand, preserve, and build upon their cultural heritage through game development.
  • Expand Global Reach: Position Geco Games Studios as a leading name in the global gaming industry by producing high-quality games that appeal to both local and international audiences.
  • Foster a Diverse Gaming Community: Create a gaming platform that encourages inclusivity, diversity, and cultural exchange, allowing players from all backgrounds to connect and engage with our games.
